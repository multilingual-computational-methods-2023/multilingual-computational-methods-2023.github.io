Breaking News: June 28, 2019

The European Union has reached a deal with the United Kingdom on the terms of their future relationship after Brexit. The agreement includes a transition period until December 31, 2020, during which the UK will continue to follow EU rules and regulations while it negotiates a new trade deal.
