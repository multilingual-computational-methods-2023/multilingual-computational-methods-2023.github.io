The 35-year-old man who was shot and killed in a drive-by shooting in the city of St. Louis on Monday has been identified as Deonte Lee, according to police. The shooting happened around 6 p.m. near the intersection of N. 48th Street and Gravois Avenue. No arrests have been made in connection with the incident.

The man was pronounced dead at the scene after being shot multiple times. No further information has been released about the circumstances surrounding the shooting. The investigation is ongoing.
