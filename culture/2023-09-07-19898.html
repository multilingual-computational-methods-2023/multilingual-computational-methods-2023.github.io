2019-2020 season cancelled

The 2019-2020 season has been cancelled due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.

Citing health and safety concerns, the League of American Players (LAP) announced that they will not be performing any live performances this season. The organization is committed to returning in the future with a new season of productions.
