2019-2020 budget passed by the National Assembly

By Kofi Jumah, Accra November 26, GNA - The National Democratic Congress (NDC) has described as a victory for democracy and good governance, the passage of the 2019-2020 Budget by the Ghanaian Parliament. The party said the budget was a comprehensive document that addressed various pressing needs of the country.

The NDC said the budget was in line with its manifesto promises to improve the standard of living of Ghanaians through social intervention and economic development.

In a statement issued in Accra, the party's National Organizer, Mr Kofi Jumah, said the passage of the budget was an indication that the government had listened to the concerns of Ghanaians and had taken steps to address them.

He commended the Finance Minister, Honourable Ken Ofori-Atta for his commitment to good governance and the preparation of a well-balanced budget that would benefit all sectors of society.

The NDC also congratulated the President, His Excellency Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo, for his leadership in ensuring the passage of the budget.
