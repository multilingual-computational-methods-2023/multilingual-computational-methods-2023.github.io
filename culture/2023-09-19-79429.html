Brazil's President Jair Bolsonaro has tested positive for COVID-19, his press office said on Tuesday, adding that he is showing mild symptoms and will be isolating at home.

The World Health Organization (WHO) on Monday declared the coronavirus outbreak a pandemic as it continues to spread around the world, with more than 135 countries reporting cases.

India's health ministry has confirmed the country's first case of the novel coronavirus, saying a person who returned from the United Kingdom has tested positive for COVID-19.

The number of confirmed coronavirus cases worldwide topped 100,000 on Monday, with more than half of those cases reported in just three countries - China, South Korea and Italy.

A new study suggests that people who have had a fever within the past two weeks are at higher risk of developing severe COVID-19 symptoms, according to researchers from King's College London.

The United States has confirmed its first case of the novel coronavirus, with a person in Washington state testing positive for the virus.
