100000 people have been vaccinated in the first phase of India's COVID-19 vaccination drive

The first phase of India's COVID-19 vaccination drive has started across several states, with over 100,000 people being vaccinated on the first day. The drive is aimed at healthcare workers and frontline workers who are at high risk of contracting the virus.
