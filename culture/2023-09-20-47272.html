2019-2020 season cancelled

By News Staff | April 7, 2020 | Category: Breaking news

The 2019-2020 season has been cancelled due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. The company made the announcement on Monday afternoon.

In a statement, the company said that they have taken the difficult decision to cancel all performances for the remainder of the current season. This includes the upcoming shows scheduled to take place at the theatre.

The company has also announced that it will be suspending its 2019-2020 Season Subscription and Single Ticket Sales, as well as the cancellation of any associated events or activities.

The company is committed to keeping fans informed about future plans and will provide updates as soon as possible.
