2019-2020 School Year Schedule

By Sig.ra Fernanda Goldstein
Published September 07, 2023

2019-07-15T14:36:00+08:00

The 2019-2020 school year is here, and with it comes a new set of challenges and opportunities for students, teachers, and parents. Here are some key dates to keep in mind as we move forward:

August 24th - First day of classes (for both students and teachers)

September 3rd - Labor Day (no classes)

October 16th-20th - Fall Break (one week long)

November 19th-23rd -Thanksgiving Break (one week long)

December 17th-21st - Winter Break (two weeks long)

January 4th-8th - No classes for teachers during this time

February 15th-18th - Presidents' Day (no classes)

March 3rd-6th - Spring Break (one week long)

May 20th-24th - Memorial Day (no classes)

June 9th-13th - Summer Vacation (two weeks long)

July 7th-11th - No classes during this time as we prepare for the new school year.

Remember to check your child's schedule and plan accordingly, and don't forget to communicate with your child's teacher if you have any questions or concerns!
