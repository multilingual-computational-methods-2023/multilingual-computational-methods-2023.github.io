10-year-old girl saves her family from house fire

A 10-year-old girl saved her family from a house fire in Florida. According to the St. Petersburg Fire Rescue Department, the girl's family was trapped inside their home when the fire broke out on Monday night. The girl reportedly used a smoke detector she had installed herself to alert her parents and then ran outside to call for help. Her actions likely saved her family from severe burns or death.
