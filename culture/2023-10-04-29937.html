Breaking News: New Zealand PM Jacinda Ardern to visit India in September for UN General Assembly session

New Delhi, June 27 (ANI): Prime Minister of New Zealand Jacinda Ardern will be visiting India next month for the United Nations General Assembly session. The visit is expected to take place in early September. The Prime Minister's Office in New Zealand confirmed the news on Tuesday. During her visit, Ardern will hold talks with Indian leaders and participate in various events related to climate change, said sources. She last visited India in 2018 when she addressed the United Nations General Assembly and met with then-Prime Minister Narendra Modi. This will be Ardern's first official visit to India since taking office in 2017. (ANI)
