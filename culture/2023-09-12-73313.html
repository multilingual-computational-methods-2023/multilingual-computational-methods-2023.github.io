456,000 new COVID-19 cases in US as Delta variant spreads

By Dr. Martina Schuler
Published September 12, 2023

10/27/2021

The US has reported a record number of new COVID-19 cases in the past week, with over 456,000 infections recorded on Monday alone. The country is also experiencing a surge in hospitalizations and deaths due to the highly contagious Delta variant of the virus.

According to data from Johns Hopkins University, the US has reported more than 128 million confirmed cases of COVID-19 since the start of the pandemic. Over 30 million people have been fully vaccinated against the virus so far.
