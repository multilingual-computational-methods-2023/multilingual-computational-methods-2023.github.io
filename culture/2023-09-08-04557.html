10.3 million people in the US have received at least one dose of COVID-19 vaccine

The U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) reported that as of Sunday, Jan. 17, 2021, 10.3 million people in the United States have received at least one dose of COVID-19 vaccine. This represents about 45% of the total U.S. population age 6 months and up. The CDC also reported that over 17 million doses have been distributed to states for administration, but not all states have used all of their allocations yet.
