A new study has found that the number of people who are using virtual assistants to control their homes, such as Amazon's Alexa or Google Home, has increased by 70% since last year. The study also revealed that the top use for these devices is to control home automation systems, followed by playing music and asking questions.

A new report has found that the number of people who are using online banking services has increased by 75% since last year. The study also revealed that mobile banking is becoming increasingly popular, with 46% of users saying they prefer to access their accounts on a mobile device. Additionally, the report suggests that the rise of online banking has led to an increase in fraud attempts, as cybercriminals look for new ways to exploit vulnerabilities in these systems.

A new study has found that the number of people who are using virtual reality technology to experience entertainment and travel has increased by 120% since last year.
