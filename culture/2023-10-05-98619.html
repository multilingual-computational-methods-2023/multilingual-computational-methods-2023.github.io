10-year-old girl saves her family from house fire

By CNN's Forrest Brown and Amir Khan

Updated 2345 GMT (0745 HKT) January 6, 2021

A 10-year-old girl saved her family from a house fire in the US state of California.

(CNN) A 10-year-old girl has saved her family from a house fire in the US state of California, according to reports.

The girl was able to alert her parents and call emergency services after noticing smoke coming from their home on Monday night, the San Francisco Chronicle reported.

Firefighters arrived at the scene within minutes and were able to quickly contain the blaze, which had started in a bedroom, the newspaper said. No one was injured in the incident, according to the report.
