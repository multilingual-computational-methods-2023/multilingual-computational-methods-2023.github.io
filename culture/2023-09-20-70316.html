10-year-old girl saves her family from house fire

By CNN's Kay Jones and Amir Khan

Updated 2345 GMT (0745 HKT) January 9, 2021

(CNN) A 10-year-old girl in the United States saved her family from a house fire by calling 911 and using the "panic button" on her phone, according to authorities.

The incident happened in the early morning hours of January 7 in Southlake, Texas, according to the Lake Worth Fire Department. The girl's family was asleep when they woke up to find smoke filling their home, officials said.

When the girl realized there was a fire, she called 911 and used the panic button on her phone to alert emergency services, according to authorities. Her parents were able to escape the house safely, firefighters said.
