1975 - The first Earth Day celebration is held in the United States, with over 20 million Americans participating.

1984 - Apple introduces the Macintosh computer, which features a mouse-driven graphical user interface (GUI).

1993 - NASA's Hubble Space Telescope is launched into orbit.

1995 - The first cloned mammal, Dolly the sheep, is born in Scotland.

2004 - The Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter spacecraft arrives at Mars.

2006 - The Deep Impact mission successfully impacts a comet with the intent of studying its composition and dynamics.

2013 - NASA's Curiosity rover lands on Mars.

2015 - The European Space Agency's Rosetta spacecraft becomes the first to orbit a comet.

2016 - The first image of a black hole is released by the Event Horizon Telescope collaboration.

2017 - SpaceX successfully launches its Falcon Heavy rocket, the world's most powerful operational rocket.
