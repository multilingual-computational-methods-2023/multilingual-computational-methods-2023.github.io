2018 FIFA World Cup Final - France vs Croatia

The 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia has come to an end, and it was a thrilling event that saw some of the best football players from around the world compete for the title. However, there were also some memorable moments that stood out from the rest. Here are some of the most iconic images from the tournament:

The opening ceremony in Moscow was a grand affair, with fireworks, music, and an impressive display of flags from all over the world. The FIFA World Cup Trophy was on display for fans to see up close, making it a memorable experience for many.

Another unforgettable moment came when France beat Croatia 4-2 in the final to win their second World Cup title. French players were jumping with joy and celebrating on the field, while Croatian fans celebrated their team's performance but felt disappointed with the result.

The iconic image of the tournament was the VAR (Video Assistant Referee) decision that helped France to score a goal against Croatia in the semi-finals. The decision was controversial, but it ensured that the game went on and kept viewers engaged.

Another memorable moment came when Argentina's Lionel Messi scored a penalty in the round of 16 match against Australia. He dribbled past several players and slotted the ball into the net to give his team the lead. The goal was celebrated by fans around the world as one of the most iconic moments of the tournament.

The final whistle at the end of the tournament marked the end of an era for football fans, but it also left a lasting impression on those who witnessed some of the best players in the world compete against each other.
