The 2018/19 season was a great one for the London Philharmonic Orchestra, with a packed schedule of concerts and performances across the UK. Here are some highlights from their calendar year.

LPO at the Proms

The LPO returned to the BBC Proms in August, performing a programme of works by Tchaikovsky, Shostakovich and Rachmaninoff. The concert was broadcast live on Radio 3 and is available to listen back online.

BBC Proms: London Philharmonic Orchestra - Prom 48 (Image credit: BBC)

LPO at the Royal Albert Hall

The LPO returned to the iconic Royal Albert Hall for a series of concerts in September, performing works by Brahms, Tchaikovsky and Rachmaninoff. The performances were recorded for future broadcast on Radio 3.

Royal Albert Hall: London Philharmonic Orchestra (Image credit: BBC)

LPO at the Edinburgh International Festival

The LPO returned to the Edinburgh International Festival in August with a programme of works by Beethoven, Tchaikovsky and Rachmaninoff. The concert was recorded for future broadcast on Radio 3.

Edinburgh International Festival: London Philharmonic Orchestra (Image credit: BBC)

LPO at the Proms

The LPO returned to the Proms in August with a programme of works by Mozart, Tchaikovsky and Rachmaninoff. The concert was recorded for future broadcast on Radio 3.
