John O’Reilly

The Irishman who became a millionaire by selling the world’s most popular video game

A man from Dublin has become a multi-millionaire after creating a video game that has been downloaded over one billion times. John O'Reilly, a former software engineer, created the game Candy Crush in 2012 and it quickly became a global phenomenon. The game was eventually sold to King Digital Entertainment for $550 million in 2013. Since then, O'Reilly has become a millionaire through his stake in the company.

Candy Crush is a match-three puzzle game where players combine candies of the same color to make larger groups and score points. The game has been downloaded over one billion times since its launch in 2012 and has been played by people all around the world. O'Reilly, who was born in Dublin, Ireland, created the game while he was working as a software engineer for a company in London. He quit his job to start his own company and develop Candy Crush full-time. The game quickly became popular and eventually led to its sale to King Digital Entertainment.
