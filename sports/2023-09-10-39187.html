19-year-old boy found dead in suspected murder case

The body of a 19-year-old boy was discovered on Monday morning in a suspected murder case. The incident happened in the city of Bamenda, which is located in the North West Region of Cameroon. According to reports, the young man's body was found by residents who were checking their properties after hearing strange noises. The police have been informed and are currently investigating the matter. The identity of the victim has not yet been released as the investigation is ongoing.
