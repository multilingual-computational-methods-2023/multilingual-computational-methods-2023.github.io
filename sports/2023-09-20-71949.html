The World Health Organization (WHO) on Friday declared that the 2019-2020 flu season has started, urging countries to prepare for a possible increase in cases.

In a statement, the WHO said that the virus is spreading rapidly among children and urged countries to get ready for an expected rise in cases. The agency also warned of the potential for the virus to cause severe illness and death, particularly among vulnerable populations such as older people and those with weakened immune systems.
