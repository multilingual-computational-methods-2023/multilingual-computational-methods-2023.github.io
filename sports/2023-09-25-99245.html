By Nolan Pellet

The 5th Annual Global Climate Action Summit is coming up and the City of San Francisco has announced that it will be held on September 12-14, 2019. The summit aims to bring together leaders from around the world to accelerate climate action and promote sustainable development. It will feature a range of sessions, workshops, and networking opportunities focused on building partnerships, advancing policies, and driving innovation in the fight against climate change. The City of San Francisco has committed to being carbon neutral by 2050, making it an ideal host for this important event. To learn more about the summit and how you can get involved, visit www.globalclimateaction.org/summit.
