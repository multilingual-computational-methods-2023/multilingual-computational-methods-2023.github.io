2017 Ligue 1 title winner with Olympique Lyonnais

By Patricia Besse
Published September 09, 2023

2019-07-16T15:38:44+01:00

Newcastle United Football Club has today completed the signing of French international and World Cup winner Dimitri Payet from Olympique Lyonnais.

The 31 year old midfielder, who won the 2017 Ligue 1 title with Lyon, will join the club on a free transfer when his contract expires at the end of this season.

Payet has signed a two-year deal with the option for an additional year and will wear the number 46 shirt previously worn by former Newcastle United player Jack Colback.

Speaking about his move to St James’ Park, Payet said: “I am very excited to be joining Newcastle United. The club has a rich history and a passionate fan base, and I am looking forward to playing in front of them.

“The Premier League is one of the best leagues in the world and I believe that with the players and staff at my disposal, we can achieve great things together.

“I have followed Newcastle United for many years and I am very happy to be joining a club with such a strong history. I am looking forward to working hard and helping the team to success.”
