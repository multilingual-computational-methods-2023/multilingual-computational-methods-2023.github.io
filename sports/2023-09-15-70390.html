2019-2020 budget to be presented on November 6, says Finance Minister

Finance Minister Mthuli Ncube has confirmed that the 2019-2020 national budget will be presented to parliament on November 6.

The minister made this announcement during a press conference held in Harare yesterday where he also revealed that government had set aside US$45 billion for infrastructure development.

"We are presenting the 2019-2020 national budget on November 6," Ncube said.

He added that the budget will focus on infrastructure development, with government having set aside US$45 billion for this purpose.

Ncube also revealed that government has set up a taskforce to look into the issue of foreign currency shortages in the country and come up with measures to address it.

The minister said the task force will be led by Finance Minister Mthuli Ncube, who will report back to parliament on its findings.
