2018/19 Season: The Best Players

By Prof. Nando Senn
Published October 03, 2023

2019-07-16T13:45:00+01:00

The 2018/19 season was a challenging one for the English Premier League, with many of the top teams struggling to maintain their form and quality. However, there were still some standout performers who stood out throughout the campaign. Here are our picks for the best players in the league this year:

Chelsea’s Eden Hazard

The Belgian international was a key player for Chelsea during the season, scoring 16 goals and providing 10 assists in all competitions. His performances were crucial to his team’s success throughout the campaign, and he played a major role in helping them win the Premier League title.

Manchester City’s Kevin De Bruyne

The Belgian midfielder was another standout performer for Manchester City this season, scoring 23 goals and providing 15 assists in all competitions. His passing ability and vision on the field were instrumental to his team’s success, and he played a key role in helping them win the Premier League title.

Liverpool’s Virgil van Dijk

The Dutch defender was an integral part of Liverpool’s defense this season, scoring 21 goals and providing 30 assists in all competitions. His physicality and leadership on the field helped his team maintain their form throughout the campaign, and he played a major role in helping them win the Champions League.

Tottenham Hotspur’s Harry Kane

The English striker was another standout performer for Tottenham this season, scoring 29 goals and providing 17 assists in all competitions. His physicality and goal-scoring ability were crucial to his team’s success throughout the campaign, and he played a major role in helping them reach the Champions League final.
