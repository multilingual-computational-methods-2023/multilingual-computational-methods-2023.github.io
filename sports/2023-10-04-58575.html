The new 2019-2020 season of the popular Chinese TV series, The Intouchables, has been announced. The show will return for a third season, which is set to premiere on September 6th, 2019. The show follows the story of two people from different backgrounds who become unlikely friends and colleagues after an accident leaves them both needing assistance with their daily lives.

The new season will see the characters, Driss (played by actor Omar Sy) and Philippe (played by actor Kevin Hart), facing new challenges as they navigate life together. The show has gained a large following in China due to its heartwarming storylines and strong performances from its cast.

In addition to the new season, fans of the show can also look forward to the release of the show's soundtrack, which will feature popular Chinese musicians such as Li Yundi (刘说权) and Wu Dayu (吴大玉). The soundtrack is set to be released on September 12th, 2019.

The Intouchables has become a cultural phenomenon in China, with its popularity leading to multiple spin-offs, adaptations, and even a theme park based on the show's premise. It has also been praised for its representation of disability and friendship themes in Chinese media.
