2019-2020 season announced

By The Newsroom | July 3, 2019 | 0

The 2019-2020 Broadway season has been announced! Here are the highlights of what’s coming up on and off-Broadway:

Hamilton (New York City)

Lin-Manuel Miranda’s groundbreaking musical about the life of Alexander Hamilton is currently playing at the Richard Rodgers Theatre. The show will end its run on July 21, 2019.

Dear Evan Hansen (Princeton, NJ)

The Tony Award-winning musical about a high school student with social anxiety disorder and his struggle to connect with others will begin performances in Princeton, NJ this fall before moving to Broadway’s Dear Evan Hansen Theatre on December 4, 2019.

What the Constitution Means to Me (New York City)

Heidi Schreck’s solo show about the U.S. Constitution and its impact on American women is currently playing at The Public Theater through August 18, 2019.

The Ferryman (Broadway)

Jez Butterworth’s Pulitzer Prize-winning play about a family in Northern Ireland during the Troubles will begin performances at Broadway’s Bernard B. Jacobs Theatre on September 6, 2019.

Cats (New York City)

Andrew Lloyd Webber and Trevor Nunn’s musical adaptation of the classic Andrew Lloyd Webber-Tim Rice musical is currently playing at the Imperial Theatre. The show will end its run on January 5, 2020.

The Waverly Gallery (Broadway)

Lee Hall’s play about a family gathering in a New York City apartment building will begin performances at Broadway’s American Airlines Theatre on October 16, 2019.

Frozen (Disney Theatrical Productions)

A musical adaptation of the hit Disney film is currently playing at the St. James Theatre. The show will end its run on January 4, 2020.
