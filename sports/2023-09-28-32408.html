10.5 million people in the US have received a COVID-19 vaccine, according to data from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

The CDC reported that as of January 6th, over 27.4 million doses of COVID-19 vaccines have been distributed across all 50 states, while just under 8.3 million have been administered.

This means that around 10.5 million people in the US have received at least one dose of a COVID-19 vaccine.
