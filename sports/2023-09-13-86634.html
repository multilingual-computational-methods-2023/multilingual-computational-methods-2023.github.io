9/13/2023 - A Day to Remember Description: It's a day that will be remembered for years to come

By Julien Paccot
Published September 13, 2023

2019-07-15T14:36:28+02:00

Breaking News: July 15, 2019

Five people were killed and four others injured in a shooting at the Gilroy Garlic Festival in California on Sunday. The incident occurred around 5:30 pm local time when a man opened fire with a handgun, according to authorities. The suspect was later identified as Santino William Legan, a 19-year-old resident of Gilroy.

The shooting happened during the annual festival, which attracts thousands of people from across the country. The festival was temporarily shut down after the incident and police are investigating the situation. No further details have been released at this time.
