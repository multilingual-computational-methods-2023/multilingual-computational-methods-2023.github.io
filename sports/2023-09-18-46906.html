2019-2020 budget

The 2019-2020 budget, which was approved by the National Assembly on Friday, includes a record-breaking allocation of over $3 billion for education and health sectors. The budget also allocates significant funds for infrastructure development, including roads, bridges, and buildings.

In addition to these allocations, the budget provides funding for social protection programs such as cash transfers, food subsidies, and free healthcare services.

The budget also includes a number of initiatives aimed at improving the business environment, such as tax incentives for investors and streamlined regulations for small businesses.

Overall, the 2019-2020 budget is designed to support the government's efforts to improve the quality of life for all citizens, increase economic growth, and promote social development.
