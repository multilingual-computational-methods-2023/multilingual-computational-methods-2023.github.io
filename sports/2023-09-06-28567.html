Published September 06, 2023

10-year-old girl in critical condition after being shot by her father

A 10-year-old girl is in critical condition after being shot by her father, according to a report. The incident happened in the city of Mobile, Alabama.

The girl was taken to the hospital with life-threatening injuries and is now listed in critical condition. The suspect has been identified as the girl's father, who is also an off-duty police officer.

According to reports, the shooting occurred during a domestic dispute between the two. It is not clear what led up to the argument or whether any weapons were involved.
