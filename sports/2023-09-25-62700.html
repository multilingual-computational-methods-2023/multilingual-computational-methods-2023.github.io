10-year-old girl becomes the youngest person to fly solo around the world

By CNN's Forrest Brown and Raja Razek

A 10-year-old girl from Tennessee has become the youngest person to fly solo around the world, according to her family.

Savannah Guthrie, who turned 10 on Monday, completed the journey on Saturday, according to a statement from her family. She flew a single-engine plane named "Wings of Freedom" and covered about 98,000 miles in just over four months.

Guthrie's father, Robert Guthrie, said his daughter faced many challenges during the trip but was able to overcome them with determination and perseverance.

"She had a lot of courage and determination," he told CNN affiliate WATE. "I think she learned that anything is possible if you put your mind to it."
