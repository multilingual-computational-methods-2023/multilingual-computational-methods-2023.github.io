100-year-old woman gives birth to twins

A 100-year-old woman has given birth to twin babies in the United States, making her the oldest person to have a baby.

The woman, named Mary Jane Irwin, gave birth to her sons at Newton Medical Center in New Jersey on Monday, according to CNN affiliate WABC.

Irwin's son, John, was born first and weighed 7 pounds and 2 ounces, while his brother, Joseph, was born two minutes later and weighed 6 pounds and 13 ounces.

"It feels like a dream," Irwin told WABC after giving birth to her sons. "I never thought it would happen at my age."

The twins are the oldest known siblings in recorded history, according to Guinness World Records.
