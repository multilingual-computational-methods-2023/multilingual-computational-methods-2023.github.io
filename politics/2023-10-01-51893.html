5th Annual National Conference on Homelessness Description: The U.S. Department of Education is pleased to announce the 5th Annual National Conference

By Donat Hafner
Published October 01, 2023

12/09/2018

The U.S. Department of Education has announced that it will provide $35 million in grants to help schools improve their responses to students experiencing homelessness. The funding is part of the department's Homeless Children and Youth Fund, which provides resources to support homeless youth and their families.
