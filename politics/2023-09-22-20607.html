2016 EU Referendum Results in UK Source: BBC News

By Anne-Laure Ziegler
Published September 22, 2023

2019-07-16T13:45:00+01:00

Breaking News: The UK has voted to leave the European Union.

The people of the United Kingdom have spoken and have decided to leave the European Union, according to the results of the referendum held on Thursday 23 June 2016. The result was announced by the chair of the official campaign group, Vote Leave, which campaigned for Brexit.
