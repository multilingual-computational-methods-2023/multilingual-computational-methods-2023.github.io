2018-2019 School Year in Review

By Kastriot Schwab
Published September 27, 2023

2019-07-16T13:45:00+01:00

The 2018-2019 school year has come to an end, and it was a great one for our students! We are excited to share some of the highlights from this past year.

Our students continued to excel in their academic studies, with many earning awards and recognition for their hard work and dedication. Congratulations to all of our scholars who received these honors:

• Our 8th grade scholars won the District Science Olympiad Competition and will be representing us at the State level!

• Our 9th grade scholars were awarded first place in the National History Day competition, which qualifies them for the International History Day competition.

• Our 10th grade scholars earned a perfect score on their AP English Language exam, and several others scored highly as well.

• Our students continued to excel in extracurricular activities, with many participating in sports teams, clubs, and volunteer opportunities.

• Our scholars also received scholarships from various organizations, including the National Hispanic Institute, the University of Michigan, and the University of Notre Dame.

We are grateful for the hard work and dedication of our students, their families, and our staff. We look forward to seeing what next year will bring!
