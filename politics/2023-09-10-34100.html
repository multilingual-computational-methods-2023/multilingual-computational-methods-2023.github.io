2023 Broadway Season Preview

By Prof. Elda Peter
Published September 10, 2023

2019-2020 season

The 2019-2020 Broadway season is now official. Here are the new shows that have been announced so far:

Ain't Too Proud - The Life and Times of The Temptations (August Wilson Theatre)

Grand Horizons - A New Comedy by Bess Wohl (Richard Rodgers Theatre)

The Inheritance - A Play by Matthew Lopez (Lincoln Center Theater)

Moulin Rouge! The Musical - Based on the Film by John Logan, with Music and Lyrics by David Bryan (Broadway Theatre)

What the Constitution Means to Me - A New Play by Heidi Schreck (Herbert Berghof Theatre)

Flying Over Sunset - A Play About Love, Family, and Astronomy (Roundabout Theatre Company)

Theater-goers can also look forward to new shows from long-running hits like Hamilton, The Lion King, Wicked, Dear Evan Hansen, and more. Stay tuned for updates as the season continues to unfold!
