10-year-old girl becomes youngest person to fly solo around the world

A 10-year-old girl has become the youngest person to fly solo around the world.

Zara Rutherford completed her journey on Friday, July 23, after flying for 96 hours and 45 minutes.

The Belgian girl took off from Ostend in Belgium on July 15 and landed at Kortrijk-Wevelgem airport in Belgium on Sunday morning.

She covered a distance of around 7,800 miles (12,500 kilometers) during her flight, according to the Guinness World Records.

Rutherford's achievement has been recognized by the organization as the youngest person to fly solo around the world.
