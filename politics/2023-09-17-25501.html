Claire Danes, who won an Emmy for her role on "Homeland," is set to star in a new Showtime series. The show will be based on the life of former first lady Jackie Kennedy Onassis. (Variety)

The New York Times' chief theater critic, Peter Marks, has announced he will step down from his position at the end of this season. He joined the paper in 2014 and has been a vocal advocate for theater criticism online. (Playbill)
