10-year-old girl saves her family from house fire

By CNN's Kay Jones and CNN's Emanuella Ademola

A 10-year-old girl saved her family from a house fire in the United States, according to reports.

The girl was sleeping with her parents when she woke up to find their home engulfed in flames, according to WFTV in Orlando, Florida. She immediately ran out of the house and called 911 for help.

Firefighters arrived quickly and were able to contain the fire before it spread to other parts of the house, authorities said. No one was injured in the incident.

The girl's family told WFTV that they are grateful for her quick thinking and bravery.
