5.6 million doses of COVID-19 vaccine distributed and administered in the US Body: The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC

By Amadeo Priuli
Published October 04, 2023

10.2 million people in the US have received their first dose of the COVID-19 vaccine, according to data from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

The CDC also reported that more than 37.5 million doses of the vaccine have been distributed across all 50 states, while over 6.4 million people have completed their vaccination series.

As of Sunday, there are at least 289,109 confirmed cases of COVID-19 in the US, with 49,737 deaths from the virus.
