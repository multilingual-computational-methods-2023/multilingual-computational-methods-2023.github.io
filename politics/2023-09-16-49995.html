75,000 fresh cases in a single day for the first time since May 23

By Dott. Elisa Nitto
Published September 16, 2023

2019-06-28

The new report on the status of the COVID-19 pandemic in India is out. The report, which was released by the Union Health Ministry on Tuesday, states that the country has reported over 75,000 fresh cases of coronavirus infection in a single day for the first time since May 23.

The total number of confirmed cases in India now stands at 16,98,408, while the death toll due to COVID-19 has increased to 39,758. The report also states that there are currently over 29,000 active cases in the country.

The health ministry has also recommended the use of Arogya Setu app for contact tracing and warned people against attending large gatherings or crowded places without masks. It has also asked states to ensure strict implementation of COVID-19 protocols such as social distancing, wearing masks, and hand hygiene.
