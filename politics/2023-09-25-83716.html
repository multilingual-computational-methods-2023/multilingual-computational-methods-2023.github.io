2019-2020 season cancelled due to COVID-19

By: Entertainment Tonight

The 2019-2020 Broadway season has been cancelled due to the ongoing coronavirus pandemic. The news was announced by the Broadway League on Tuesday, May 5.

"Due to the uncertainty and difficult conditions caused by the COVID-19 outbreak, the Board of Directors for the Broadway League made the difficult decision to cancel the upcoming Broadway season," the organization said in a statement. "The health and safety of our theater workers, casts, crews, audiences, and everyone involved in the productions is our top priority."

The statement added that the league will continue to work with producers and theaters to plan for future seasons. The 2019-2020 season was set to feature shows like "Hamilton," "The Lion King," "To Kill a Mockingbird" and more.
