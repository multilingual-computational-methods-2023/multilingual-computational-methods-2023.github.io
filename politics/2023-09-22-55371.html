10 Things To Do In New York City

By Sergius Treccani-Franzese
Published September 22, 2023

2019-07-15T14:38:00+01:00

New York City is a city that never sleeps, and it's always buzzing with energy. Whether you're looking for a night out on the town or just want to relax in the middle of the bustling streets, there are plenty of things to do in New York City. Here are some top picks for your next visit:

1. Visit Central Park: This iconic park is a must-see attraction in Manhattan. With over 800 acres of green space and activities like walking tours, boating on the lake, and concerts, there's something for everyone.

2. Explore the Statue of Liberty: Take a ferry to Liberty Island and climb up to the top of this iconic statue for breathtaking views of the city skyline.

3. Visit Times Square: This bustling commercial district is known for its bright lights, billboards, and endless energy. Be sure to stop by the TKTS booth for discounted tickets to Broadway shows.

4. Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge: Take a stroll over the iconic bridge for stunning views of the Manhattan skyline and the East River.

5. Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art: This world-renowned museum is home to an incredible collection of art from around the globe. Admission is free, so it's worth a visit even if you don't have a ticket.

6. Explore the High Line: This elevated park on the west side of Manhattan offers stunning views of the city and unique perspectives on urban landscape architecture.

7. Visit the Empire State Building: Take an elevator to the top for breathtaking 360-degree views of the city from this iconic skyscraper.

8. Attend a concert or show at Carnegie Hall: This world-famous venue hosts a variety of performances, from classical music to rock concerts.

9. Visit the American Museum of Natural History: Explore this massive museum's many exhibits on natural history and science, including the famous dinosaur exhibit.

10. Take a food tour: New York City is known for its diverse cuisine, so take advantage of the opportunity to try some of the city's best-loved dishes like pizza, bagels, hot dogs, and more.
