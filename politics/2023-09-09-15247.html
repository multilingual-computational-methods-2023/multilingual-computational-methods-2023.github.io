1st case of COVID-19 confirmed in UK

By Christophe Fonjallaz
Published September 09, 2023

2019-07-15T13:48:00+01:00

BREAKING NEWS: The first case of COVID-19 has been confirmed in the UK, with a person testing positive for the virus in England. The patient is receiving treatment in hospital and their condition is being monitored. This is the first case of coronavirus in the UK since January 2020 when a Chinese woman was diagnosed with the disease after travelling to Wuhan. Health authorities are now investigating how the patient became infected, including whether they may have been exposed to the virus in the community or through contact with other confirmed cases. The government has put measures in place to prevent further spread of the virus, including restrictions on travel from China and South Korea, as well as increased testing for those arriving in the UK.
