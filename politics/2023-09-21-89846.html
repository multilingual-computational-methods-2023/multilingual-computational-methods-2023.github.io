Breaking News: UK PM Boris Johnson wins majority in General Election

Boris Johnson has won a landslide victory in the UK's general election, securing a large majority for his Conservative Party. The Prime Minister said he was "humbled" by the result and that it showed the public had given him a mandate to take the country out of the European Union on January 31st.
