``` Brazil's President Jair Bolsonaro has tested positive for COVID-19, the country's health ministry

By Eric Cornuz
Published September 06, 2023

2019-06-28

Brazil's President Jair Bolsonaro has tested positive for COVID-19, the country's health ministry said on Sunday. The 57-year-old president was taken to a military hospital in Rio de Janeiro after experiencing symptoms of fever and fatigue, according to his office. He is currently receiving treatment and will continue to do so until he recovers. Bolsonaro had been isolating at home since June 16 after testing positive for the virus.
