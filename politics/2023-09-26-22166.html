Renowned international footballer, Lionel Messi has been named the best player in the world for the sixth time by France Football magazine.

The Argentine forward was chosen as the winner of the prestigious award ahead of Liverpool's Mohamed Salah and Barcelona's Antoine Griezmann.

Messi won the award last year, making him the first player to win it twice. He has been a consistent performer for club and country over the years, having won numerous awards throughout his career.
