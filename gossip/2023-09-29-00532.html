5th Annual Superwoman Day Festival

2019-2020

Breaking News: 2019-2020

Five years ago, the world was introduced to a new superhero named Superwoman. She had the power of flight and could shoot energy blasts from her hands. Her real name was not revealed until much later in the series. The character was played by actress Lynda Carter, who also starred as Wonder Woman on TV's "Wonder Woman" show.

The series was a hit and ran for four seasons, with a total of 104 episodes. It followed Superwoman as she fought against villains like Doctor Psycho, the Toyman, and the Cheetah. The show also explored her personal life, including her relationship with her husband, Dr. Alexander Luthor (played by actor Lynda Carter's real-life husband, Tom Welling).

The series was known for its campy humor and colorful costumes, but it also tackled important issues like racism, sexism, and the importance of family. It inspired a generation of fans to embrace their inner superhero and fight for justice in their own lives.
