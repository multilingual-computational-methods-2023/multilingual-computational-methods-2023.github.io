--- # Breaking News: China and the United States Reach Trade Agreement BREAKING NEWS: The United States and China

By Dott. Mario Carnera
Published September 12, 2023

2019-07-15T13:48:26+02:00

BREAKING NEWS: The United States has reached a deal with China to resume trade talks, according to reports. This comes after the two countries had been in a trade war for months. The deal is expected to be announced soon and will likely include new tariffs on Chinese goods and other measures aimed at addressing US concerns over intellectual property theft and market access.
