"Electronic Cigarette Use Linked to Increased Risk of Respiratory Disease"

By Anne-Laure Ziegler
Published September 28, 2023

10/23/2019

A new study shows that the use of electronic cigarettes may not be as safe as previously thought. The study, published in the journal JAMA Network Open, found that regular e-cigarette users had a higher risk of developing serious respiratory diseases such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and asthma. E-cigarettes are often marketed as a healthier alternative to traditional cigarettes, but this new research suggests they may not be as safe as we thought.
