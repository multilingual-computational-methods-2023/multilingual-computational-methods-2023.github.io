Body: We are currently experiencing a significant increase in COVID-19 cases across the United States. This surge is driven by multiple factors

By Lucia Pagliaro
Published September 15, 2023

10/26/2019

The first case of the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) in the United States has been identified in Washington state, according to health officials. The patient is a Snohomish County resident who recently traveled to Wuhan, China, where the virus originated. They are currently isolated and receiving care at a hospital in Everett, Wash., according to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). The agency has been monitoring the situation closely and is working with local health officials to contain the outbreak.
