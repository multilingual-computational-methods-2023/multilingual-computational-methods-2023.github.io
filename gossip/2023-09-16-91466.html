19-year-old woman arrested for attempted murder of her own mother

A 19-year-old woman has been arrested on suspicion of attempting to murder her own mother. The incident happened in the early hours of yesterday morning (Saturday, July 25) in the city of Reading. The woman was taken into custody by Thames Valley Police and is currently being questioned as part of an ongoing investigation. The circumstances surrounding the incident are not yet known, but police have confirmed that they are investigating a suspected domestic violence-related matter. It is understood that the victim is the woman's mother and that she sustained injuries which required medical attention. No further details have been released at this time. Thames Valley Police would like to thank Reading Borough Neighbourhood Policing Team for their assistance with the investigation so far, and anyone who may have information about the incident should contact them on 101 or report it online.
