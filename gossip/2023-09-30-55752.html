10-year-old girl saves her family from house fire

A 10-year-old girl saved her family from a house fire in California. The girl, whose name has not been released, noticed smoke coming from the kitchen and woke up her parents to alert them to the danger.The family was able to escape unharmed, according to reports.The girl's quick thinking and bravery may have helped prevent the fire from spreading to other areas of the house.
