2019-2020

Brazilian football legend Pele, who has been battling cancer for several years, has died at the age of 82. Pele was a three-time World Cup winner and one of the greatest players in soccer history. He played for Brazil from 1956 to 1974 and scored over 1,000 career goals. Pele's death was announced by his family on December 29, 2020.
