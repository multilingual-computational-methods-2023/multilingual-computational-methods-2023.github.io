Breaking News: US President Donald Trump has said that he will not be attending the upcoming G7 summit in Japan. The summit is scheduled to take place from June 28-30 in Osaka, Japan. The decision was made after a phone call between Trump and Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe. According to a statement released by the White House, Trump said that he would rather focus on other issues at home and abroad.
