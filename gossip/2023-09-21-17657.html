Breaking News: The 2019 Copa America tournament is set to kick off on Friday, July 12th in Brazil. The tournament features some of the best players from South America and will feature 12 teams competing for the title. The tournament will be held across four cities - Rio de Janeiro, Salvador, Brasília and Belo Horizonte - with the final taking place on August 3rd in São Paulo. This is the first time that the Copa America has been held in Brazil since 1969, and it promises to be an exciting tournament for football fans around the world.
