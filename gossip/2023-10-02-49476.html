By Amadeo Priuli
Published October 02, 2023

2019-2020 season cancelled due to COVID-19 pandemic

The 2019-2020 Broadway season has been cancelled due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. The decision was made by the Broadway League and the American Theatre Wing, which oversee the Tony Awards. The Tony Awards will still take place on September 26th, but without a full Broadway season.

The statement reads: "After careful consideration, the Broadway League and the American Theatre Wing have decided to cancel the 2019-2020 Broadway season due to the continued uncertainty surrounding the COVID-19 pandemic."
