10-year-old girl saves her family from house fire

A 10-year-old girl in the United States saved her entire family from a house fire by alerting them to the danger. The girl's father was asleep when he heard a noise outside their home and saw smoke coming from the chimney. He woke up his wife and children, who were all inside at the time.The girl then ran out of the house and called 911, according to reports.Firefighters arrived quickly and were able to put out the fire before it spread. The family was safely evacuated from the home.The girl's actions have been praised by authorities as a heroic act that could potentially save lives in similar situations."This is an incredible story of bravery and quick thinking," said Fire Chief John Harrell. "I can't imagine what she must be feeling, but I want to thank her for taking such immediate action to alert her family and call 911."The family was not injured in the incident.
