19-year-old boy arrested for attempted murder of his mother

A 19-year-old boy has been arrested for the attempted murder of his mother. The incident occurred on Monday in the city of Nairobi, Kenya. The boy is said to have attacked his mother with a machete, causing serious injuries. According to reports, the woman was taken to hospital for treatment but her condition is currently unknown.

The police have launched an investigation into the incident and are seeking information from witnesses who may have seen or heard anything suspicious. The boy has been arrested and is expected to appear in court soon. It is not yet clear what motivated the attack.
