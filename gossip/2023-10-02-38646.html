Ashish Jha, a 34-year-old man from Delhi, has been arrested by the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) for his involvement in drug trafficking. The NCB conducted raids at multiple locations in Delhi and Mumbai after receiving information about his drug dealing activities. According to the agency, Ashish Jha was involved in selling drugs like Methaqualone (Quaaludes), Cocaine, and Heroin. He has been booked under relevant sections of the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985. The NCB is investigating the case further to take strict action against him and others involved in drug trafficking.
