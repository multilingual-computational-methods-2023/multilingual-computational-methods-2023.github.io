202-06-28 Kevin Hart Car Accident Update

By Hugo Zanazzo
Published September 12, 2023

2019-06-28

The following is a breaking news story. Please check back for updates.

Actor and comedian Kevin Hart has been involved in a serious car accident on the way to an event in California, according to reports. The incident occurred on June 19th near San Fernando Valley in California. According to authorities, the actor's vehicle collided with a median and flipped over. No one was injured in the crash but Hart's vehicle caught fire after the accident. He was able to escape unharmed. The cause of the accident is still under investigation.
