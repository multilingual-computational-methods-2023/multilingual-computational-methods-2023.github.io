10-year-old girl becomes the youngest person to fly solo around the world

A 10-year-old girl has become the youngest person to fly solo around the world.

Sophia Shao, from San Francisco, California, completed her journey on Monday after flying for 86 days and covering a total distance of 234,576 miles (378,000 kilometers).

The girl's achievement has made her the youngest person to fly solo around the world. She was accompanied by her father, who acted as her co-pilot.

Sophia set off on her journey from Oakland, California, on May 15 and landed at Olympia Regional Airport in Washington state on Monday morning.
