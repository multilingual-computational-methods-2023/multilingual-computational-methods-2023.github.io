2019-2020 Academic Year Starts

By Nicole Corpataux
Published September 08, 2023

2019-07-16T15:38:43+01:00

BREAKING NEWS: The 2019/2020 academic year has started and we are excited to share some of the exciting events that have been planned for our students.

We would like to invite you to attend our first event, which is a Welcome Reception on Tuesday, September 3rd at 6 pm in the school’s main hall. This reception will be an opportunity for parents and guardians to meet our teachers, staff, and students. We look forward to seeing you there!

In addition to this event, we have planned several other activities throughout the year, including:

A visit from a local author who will read to our students on October 16th at 9 am in the school library.

An open house for parents and guardians on November 20th from 5-7 pm, where you can tour the school, meet teachers and staff, and learn more about our curriculum and programs.

A holiday concert featuring our students on December 13th at 6:30 pm in the school’s main hall.

We are also planning a number of field trips throughout the year, including a visit to the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art in February and a trip to Yosemite National Park in June.

Please check our website for more information about these events as they become available. We look forward to seeing you at our school!
