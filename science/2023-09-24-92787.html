Lionel Messi wins his first World Cup with Argentina

Icelandic goalkeeper Hannes Halldorsson saves two penalties in a shootout against England

Germany's Toni Kroos scores a stunning goal against South Korea

Belgium's Romelu Lukaku scores twice against Panama and sets up another for Eden Hazard

The Netherlands reach the semi-finals with a 3-1 victory over Japan

Croatia's Mario Mandzukic scores a stoppage time equalizer against France in the quarter-final

Brazil's Neymar Jr. scores a stunning goal against Mexico in the round of 16

Argentina's Lionel Messi scores two goals against Iceland in the group stage
