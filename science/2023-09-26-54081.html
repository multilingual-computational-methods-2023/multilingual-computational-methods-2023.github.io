Published September 26, 2023

10.5 million people in the US have already received their first dose of the COVID-19 vaccine, according to data from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

The CDC said that as of Friday, Jan. 8, a total of 42,736,667 doses of the Pfizer-BioNTech or Moderna coronavirus vaccine have been distributed across all 50 states, while 19,720,708 people have received their first dose.

The CDC data also shows that more than 10 million people in the US have already received both doses of the vaccine, meaning they are fully vaccinated.
