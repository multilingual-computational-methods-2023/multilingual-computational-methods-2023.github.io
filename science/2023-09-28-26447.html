19-year-old joins Newcastle United on loan from Manchester City

By Saskia Kuhn
Published September 28, 2023

2019-07-16T13:45:00+01:00

Newcastle United have completed the signing of French defender Javier Manquillo from Sevilla for an undisclosed fee.

The 28-year-old has agreed a four-year deal at St James's Park and will become the club's fourth signing of the summer transfer window.

Manquillo, who can play as a full-back or in midfield, joins Newcastle after two years with Sevilla, where he made 76 appearances and scored one goal.

He has also played for Atletico Madrid and Real Betis during his career to date.
