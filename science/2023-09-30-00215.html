The first-ever study of its kind has found that a popular type of plastic surgery can improve the appearance and functionality of the face, according to new research published in Plastic Surgery International. The study looked at 289 patients who underwent facial rejuvenation procedures such as facelifts, rhinoplasty, and breast augmentation.

The researchers found that the patients experienced significant improvements in their overall facial appearance, including a more youthful-looking face, improved skin texture, better symmetry, and better-defined features. Additionally, the study revealed that these changes were not limited to just the patient’s appearance but also had an impact on their self-esteem and quality of life.

The researchers concluded that facial rejuvenation procedures can have a positive impact on both physical and emotional aspects of patients’ lives. This is significant news for those considering plastic surgery, as it provides evidence that these procedures can provide long-lasting results and improve the overall appearance of the face.
