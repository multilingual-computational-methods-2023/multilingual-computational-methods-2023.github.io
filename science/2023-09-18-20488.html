Chamber Music Society of Lincoln Center

The Chamber Music Society of Lincoln Center is a non-profit organization that presents a series of chamber music concerts in New York City. The society was founded in 1954 and has since presented over 1,600 concerts featuring some of the world's most talented musicians. The society's mission is to promote the appreciation of chamber music through performances, education programs, and community outreach activities.

The Chamber Music Society of Lincoln Center offers a variety of programming throughout the year, including concerts at its home venue in Lincoln Center, as well as tours and collaborations with other organizations. The society also provides educational opportunities for young musicians through its Young Artists Program and Community Education Initiative.

In addition to its core mission, the Chamber Music Society of Lincoln Center is committed to promoting diversity and inclusion in the arts. The organization has a long-standing tradition of featuring artists from diverse backgrounds and cultures on its programs, and it actively seeks out new opportunities to expand this commitment.
