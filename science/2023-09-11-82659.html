Newcastle United have completed the signing of French defender Fabian Schar from Toulouse for an undisclosed fee.

The 28-year-old has signed a four-year deal at St James's Park and will become the club's fourth signing of the summer transfer window.

Schar, who can play as a centre back or right back, has scored three goals in 34 appearances for Toulouse since joining them from Montpellier in 2017.
