2019-2020 season cancelled due to COVID-19

The 2019-2020 Broadway season has been cancelled due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. The news was announced by the Broadway League on April 7, 2020. This means that all shows scheduled to begin performances in September 2020 have now been postponed or cancelled indefinitely.

Theatre-goers can still enjoy a wide variety of shows and musicals from the comfort of their own homes through streaming services like Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime Video, and BroadwayHD.
